Conference Producer & Writer

  • San Francisco, CA
  • Full Time
  • Experienced
  • Share

LAUNCH is a media company focused on startups, technology and entrepreneurship. We host in-person events (LAUNCH Festival), an incubator, a podcast (This Week in Startups) and a research service (LAUNCH Ticker).

We’re looking for a clever, fast, organized, and passionate conference producer and writer here in San Francisco.

Our events include the LAUNCH Festival, now in its eighth year, which drew over 12,000 attendees in 2015. Other events include LAUNCH Mobile, LAUNCH Scale, and the LAUNCH Hackathon. We are also preparing for our third class of the LAUNCH Incubator.

Online we produce the LAUNCH Ticker and This Week in Startups, which draw hundreds of thousands of viewers monthly.

Job responsibilities include:

1. Creating editorial themes for our conferences.

2. Inviting speakers and startups to our events.

3. Writing and editing our emails, website copy and other collateral.

4. Documenting our learning from our incubator.

We’re looking for someone with three or more years in event production and/or editorial. This is a full-time position in San Francisco (no remote).

We’re a small team of passionate individuals, so you can make a real impact on the future of startups that debut at our events, which have included Dropbox, Yammer, FitBit, Mint, Space Monkey, Uber, and hundreds of others over the past seven years.

If interested, please answer these four fun questions so we can get to know you, and we’ll be in touch.

1. What’s the best event you’ve ever been to and why?

2. What do you think is the most important aspect of throwing a great event?

3. Tell us about a challenge you had as a child, or young adult, and how you overcame it.

4. What appeals to you about this opportunity?
