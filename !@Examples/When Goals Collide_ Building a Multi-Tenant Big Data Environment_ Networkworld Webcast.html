When Goals Collide: Building a Multi-Tenant Big Data Environment

Wednesday, June 24, 2015 01:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time

Does your organization face competitive or regulatory imperatives that require higher quality big data analysis in ever shorter amounts of time, even as data volumes explode?
Are you being pressed to deploy new applications and innovative application frameworks such as Hadoop, Spark, and NoSQL databases?

Are you suffering from cluster sprawl - a multiplicity of isolated, inefficient application silos spread across the organization as new compute clusters are set up to run specific applications?
Please join us to learn how sharing of compute resources across an efficient multi-tenant environment can help your organization realize competitive advantage through
-Accelerated time to results performance

-Increased utilization of hardware resources

-Improved management efficiencies

We will also describe selected customer success stories and also provide an introduction to IBM Platform Application Service Controller, the new IBM Software Defined Infrastructure solution for efficient resource scheduling and workflow management for long-running born-in-the-cloud services including Spark, MongoDB and Cassandra.
